Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is a classic dish originated from italy. It is known as Garfield's favorite food and is most likely your favortie too. here's how to prepare it. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • Tomato paste
  • Ground beef
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. lubricate your lasagna pan
  2. Add the pasta, followed by the paste and beef mix
  3. repeat the process three times
  4. cover with bechamel and cheese
  5. put in a preheated oven for 45 mins
  6. enjoy!
