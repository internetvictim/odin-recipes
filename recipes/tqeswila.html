Tqeswila

Description

This is the all-time classic for world-renown chef nazim. its really a simple recipe when it comes down to it, but its simplicity is its magic. onions, tuna, peppers, and maybe tomatoes and eggs. and voila!

Ingredients

  • onions
  • olive oil
  • bell peppers
  • tuna,chicken or ground beef
  • tomatoes
  • eggs

Steps

  1. add spiced up onions to oiled up pan
  2. leave for a few minutes at low heat
  3. add the peppers and tomatoes
  4. add the meat
  5. add eggs
  6. eat
